Coronavirus test: confirm your details

Use this form to confirm your details for a coronavirus (COVID-19) test.

Who can use this form

You can use this form if you have either:

  • been sent a home test kit
  • been asked to use it by staff at a test site
  • been invited to use it by an employer or NHS trust

If other people you live with have also been asked to use this form, we'll need their details, too. They can enter their details on this form after you've registered yourself (or you can do it for them, with their permission).

    What’s your ethnic group?

    This will help us understand how coronavirus is affecting people of different ethnic backgrounds.

    What's your mobile phone number?

    We’ll text your registration confirmation and test results to this number.

    What's your email address?

    We’ll email your registration confirmation and test results to this email address.

    Make sure you’re happy for your test results to go to this email address. Do not use a work or shared email address unless you’re comfortable with others possibly seeing your results.

    Do you know your NHS number?

    It's OK if you do not. We can still confirm your details without it.

    Your NHS number is a 10 digit number, like 485 777 3456. You can find it on any letter the NHS has sent you, on a prescription, or by logging in to a GP practice online service.

    Do you have an NHS COVID-19 app reference code?

    The app is not yet available across the whole of the UK.

    Please select ‘No, I do not have a reference’.

    Which test are you having?
    Select whether you are taking a test at home or at a test site.

    Having trouble? Call the coronavirus testing helpline on 0300 303 2713. Lines are open from 7am to 11pm every day. It's free to call from any mobile or landline phone.
